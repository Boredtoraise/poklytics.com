Currently GGPoker and PokerStars hand histories. We're adding 888poker, WPT Global, and more soon. Lifetime members get all future site support automatically.
What does "Lifetime Deal" mean exactly?
Pay once, get access to every tool we build — forever. No monthly fees, no renewals. This includes Leak Report, Tournament Report, HandIQ, Daily Spot, and any future tools. This price is only available for the first 100 founding members.
Is my hand history data safe?
Yes. Your files are processed on our server, the report is generated, and the raw hand history is deleted immediately. We never store your hand data or share it with anyone.
