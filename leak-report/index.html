Leaks Found

Each leak is ranked by severity and estimated monthly cost to your bankroll.
Fold to 3-Bet Too High
Critical
Your: 72%  |  Optimal: 50–58%  |  Est. Cost: ~$180/mo
Opponents can exploit you by 3-betting light and printing money every time you fold. This is your most expensive leak.
How to Fix
Widen your 4-bet bluff range from BTN/CO. Add A5s, A4s, suited connectors to your defending range. Stop folding to positional 3-bets without a plan.
C-Bet Turn Too Aggressive
Critical
Your: 68%  |  Optimal: 40–48%  |  Est. Cost: ~$110/mo
You're firing the turn too often with air on unfavorable runouts. Opponents with hands are calling you down easily.
How to Fix
Only barrel with strong made hands, nut draws, and blockers. Give up on wet turn cards that miss your range. Ask: does this card improve my range or theirs?
3-Bet Frequency Too Low
Warning
Your: 5.1%  |  Optimal: 8–10%
You're playing too passively preflop, missing value with premium hands and letting opponents steal too cheaply. Fix the fold-to-3bet leak first — it will help you here too.

Don't have a .zip file? Here's how to export
GGPoker
  1. Go to PokerCraft (pokercraft.com or in-app)
  2. Select Hand History tab
  3. Choose date range & game type
  4. Click Download — you'll get a .zip file
PokerStars
  1. Open PokerStars desktop client
  2. Go to Settings → Playing History → Request Hand History
  3. Select date range
  4. Check your email — Stars sends a .zip download link
